SP7001 VLATTICE - Visible Lattice Points

题意

求：

3+3∑i=1n∑j=1n[gcd⁡(i,j)==1]+∑i=1n∑j=1n∑k=1n[gcd⁡(i,j,k)==1]3+3\sum_{i=1}^n\sum_{j=1}^n[\gcd(i,j)==1]+\sum_{i=1}^n\sum_{j=1}^n\sum_{k=1}^n[\gcd(i,j,k)==1] 3+3i=1∑n​j=1∑n​[gcd(i,j)==1]+i=1∑n​j=1∑n​k=1∑n​[gcd(i,j,k)==1]

分析

没啥分析的，直接考虑化简

对于三元的部分有：

∑i=1n∑j=1n∑k=1n[gcd⁡(i,j,k)==1]=∑i=1n∑j=1n∑k=1n∑d∣gcd⁡(i,j,k)μ(d)=∑i=1nμ(i)⌊ni⌋3\begin{aligned} \sum_{i=1}^n\sum_{j=1}^n\sum_{k=1}^n[\gcd(i,j,k)==1]&=\sum_{i=1}^n\sum_{j=1}^n\sum_{k=1}^n\sum_{d|\gcd(i,j,k)}\mu(d)\\ &=\sum_{i=1}^n\mu(i)\left\lfloor\frac ni\right\rfloor^3 \end{aligned} i=1∑n​j=1∑n​k=1∑n​[gcd(i,j,k)==1]​=i=1∑n​j=1∑n​k=1∑n​d∣gcd(i,j,k)∑​μ(d)=i=1∑n​μ(i)⌊in​⌋3​

对于二元的部分有：

3∑i=1n∑j=1n[gcd⁡(i,j)==1]=3∑i=1n∑j=1n∑d∣gcd⁡(i,j)μ(d)=3∑i=1nμ(i)⌊ni⌋2\begin{aligned} 3\sum_{i=1}^n\sum_{j=1}^n[\gcd(i,j)==1]&=3\sum_{i=1}^n\sum_{j=1}^n\sum_{d|\gcd(i,j)}\mu(d)\\ &=3\sum_{i=1}^n\mu(i)\left\lfloor\frac ni\right\rfloor^2 \end{aligned} 3i=1∑n​j=1∑n​[gcd(i,j)==1]​=3i=1∑n​j=1∑n​d∣gcd(i,j)∑​μ(d)=3i=1∑n​μ(i)⌊in​⌋2​

所以可以稍微整理一下：

ans=3+3∑i=1n∑j=1n[gcd⁡(i,j)==1]+∑i=1n∑j=1n∑k=1n[gcd⁡(i,j,k)==1]=3+3∑i=1nμ(i)⌊ni⌋2+∑i=1nμ(i)⌊ni⌋3=3+∑i=1nμ(i)⌊ni⌋×(3⌊ni⌋+⌊ni⌋2)\begin{aligned} ans&=3+3\sum_{i=1}^n\sum_{j=1}^n[\gcd(i,j)==1]+\sum_{i=1}^n\sum_{j=1}^n\sum_{k=1}^n[\gcd(i,j,k)==1]\\ &=3+3\sum_{i=1}^n\mu(i)\left\lfloor\frac ni\right\rfloor^2+\sum_{i=1}^n\mu(i)\left\lfloor\frac ni\right\rfloor^3\\ &=3+\sum_{i=1}^n\mu(i)\left\lfloor\frac ni\right\rfloor\times(3\left\lfloor\frac ni\right\rfloor+\left\lfloor\frac ni\right\rfloor^2) \end{aligned} ans​=3+3i=1∑n​j=1∑n​[gcd(i,j)==1]+i=1∑n​j=1∑n​k=1∑n​[gcd(i,j,k)==1]=3+3i=1∑n​μ(i)⌊in​⌋2+i=1∑n​μ(i)⌊in​⌋3=3+i=1∑n​μ(i)⌊in​⌋×(3⌊in​⌋+⌊in​⌋2)​

然后数论分块一下就可以了
