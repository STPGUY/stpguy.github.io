n−ala+1=m−blb+1\frac{n-al}{a+1}=\frac{m-bl}{b+1} a+1n−al​=b+1m−bl​

然后就可以一同化简猛如虎：

nb+n−abl−al=am+m−abl−bln+nb−al=m+ma−blnb+bl−ma−al=m−nb(n+l)−a(m+l)=m−n\begin{aligned} nb+n\cancel{-abl}-al&=am+m\cancel{-abl}-bl\\ n + nb - al &= m + ma - bl\\ nb + bl -ma-al&=m-n\\ b(n+l)-a(m+l)&=m-n \end{aligned} nb+n−abl​−aln+nb−alnb+bl−ma−alb(n+l)−a(m+l)​=am+m−abl​−bl=m+ma−bl=m−n=m−n​
