##离散傅里叶变换的逆变换

\begin{align*} \sum_{i=0}^{n-1}b_i{\omega_n}^{-ki}&=\sum_{i=0}^{n-1}{\omega_n}^{-ki}\sum_{j=0}^{n-1}{\omega_n}^{ij}a_j\\ &=\sum_{j=0}^{n-1}a_j\sum_{i=0}^{n-1}{\omega_n}^{-ki}{\omega_n}^{ij}\\ &=\sum_{j=0}^{n-1}a_j\sum_{i=0}^{n-1}{\omega_n}^{i(j-k)} \end{align*}
